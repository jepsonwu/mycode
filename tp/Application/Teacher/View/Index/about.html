About-Us

Tollk is anonline English Language Teaching (ELT) platform which is exploited by Hangzhou GuaJiao Technology company in China, and creates thousands of part-time jobs for native English speakersas an online spoken English teacher. You want to learn how to speak in perfect English, Tollk provides such a chance for you, and of course teachers will get the correspondingrewardby answering the incoming calls from students.

The Meaning of Tollk

The pronunciation of Tollk is “təuk” like “talk” ,which means it is an educational tool of instant chat surrounding with “Speak,” we build the platform for teachers and students to communicate with each other. Tollk is paid for using, and we hope this platform willspread Spoken English to more English learners, and givemore conditions to improveyour English. And you will obtain reward for teaching by Tollk.
